Groundsat

Phase 4 Ground is developing a Groundsat


Phase 4 Ground is working on a Groundsat, which is a satellite simulation intended for terrestrial deployment.

Not only does this provide a way to test and develop open source solutions, but it also provides a way to collaborate on the communications payload required for the spacecraft. Phase 4 Ground and Phase 4 Space collaborate and complement each other primarily through the air interface and Groundsat design.
